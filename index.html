Experience Luxury and Convenience at Hotel SKS Grand Palace in Vrindavan

Hotel SKS Grand Palace, a new hotel in Vrindavan, has opened its doors and is ready to host the most lavish events and ceremonies for you and your loved ones. This hotel in Vrindavan showcases marvellous amenities alongside spacious and luxurious venues, making it the perfect choice for your stay in Mathura and Vrindavan. The hotel is centrally located between Mathura and Vrindavan, with close proximity to all the major temples and tourist attractions of Mathura district, including Banke Bihari temple, Prem Mandir, Dwarkadhish temple, Krishna Janam Bhumi, Barsana, Gokul, Govardhan, Raman Reti, and Tatiya Sthan.

EXCEPTIONAL SERVICES AT OUR HOTEL IN THE HEART OF VRINDAVAN

Our hotel in Vrindavan offers comfortable and shiny Suites, Super-Deluxe and Deluxe rooms with a total capacity of hosting 144 guests. The hotel is the tallest in Mathura city and features beautiful banquets that are accessible through our gold standard Schindler elevator, stairs and wheelchairs. We also have the largest in-house parking in Mathura city, ensuring that our guests have a convenient and hassle-free experience.

Our in-house fine dine restaurant, Dawat, one of the best restaurants in Vrindavan offers a multi-cuisine menu and serves the most decadent and mouth-watering North-Indian and Eastern dishes in the whole of Brij Bhoomi. It caters to both national and international clientele visiting Mathura and Vrindavan.

SKS GRAND PALACE WEDDING VENUES IN VRINDAVAN

SKS Grand Pearl Hall is the pearl of our property and is best suited to make your events memorable just like natural beautiful pearls. The venue is located at the lower ground (LG) level and has a vibrant and pure environment. SKS Grand Emerald Hall is a venue with an open balcony, ideal for breezy brunches under the blue clouds or moonlight dinners. SKS Grand Palace, with its 50-foot ceilings and Roman-style building structure, is the top priority for politicians, celebrities, and industrialists to host events. The venue is a "facade of grandeur" and is surrounded by a lush green lawn with imported Bottle Palms and Washingtonias plants, as well as a modern square to help you soak up some sun while sipping on refreshing drinks.
