Tajmahal

It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. The monument is known for its exquisite beauty, including the intricate carvings, the use of precious stones, and the stunning white marble dome.

Red fort

It was built in the 16th century by Mughal emperor Akbar and served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors until the capital was shifted to Delhi. The fort is known for its impressive architecture, intricate carvings, and its role in the history of India, including the imprisonment of Shah Jahan, who built the Taj Mahal.

Distance: 76 KM
